About us






WHO ARE WE??

Rate My Restaurant is a webpage that deals with rating hotels and restaurant.This idea was born when we saw that people had struggles when getting good hotels when they visit new and unfamiliar places.For example Take this scenario where you have gone to a new place and there is no resaurant that you know, this is where this page comes in handy where you can log in your current location and get the restaurants near you and the ratings meaning that your satisfaction using this webapp is 98%.





WHAT DO WE DO??

Here at Rate My Restaurant normal people log in and rate hotel and restaurants in their location to better the experience of this hotels.The restaurants will be made better through the comments they receive from the people through the website. The website works through people creating an account which is free and then loging in. After the login the user can search the hotel or restaurant the visit it. after the visit the user will rate the hotel or restaurant and can also comment About the experience there,which can be service or the food etc. By this they will help others.
